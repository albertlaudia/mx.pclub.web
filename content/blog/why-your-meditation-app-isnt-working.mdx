## The problem with "10 minutes of mindfulness"

Most meditation apps open with a 10-minute session. They assume you have 10 minutes. They assume you want silence. They assume you're in a state where sitting still is what you need.

**None of those assumptions are true for the people who need the help most.**

The person with racing thoughts at 2am doesn't need to "observe their breath." They need to *stop having* racing thoughts. The person with pre-meeting anxiety doesn't want a 20-minute body scan. They want to feel less like throwing up. The person with burnout doesn't want to *sit and be present with their feelings*. They want to feel something other than numb.
